Black Eyed Peas recipe

* Soak a pound of black eyed peas overnight 
* Dice a white onion and throw it in a Dutch oven with a little olive oil and a few dashes of salt
* Slice up a smoked beef sausage link and toss it in
* Let that cook on medium-high heat for a few minutes until the onions start to brown 
* Add a little oil if necessary
* Throw in the black eyed peas
* Add a few bay leaves
* Pour seven cups of water over the beans
* Bring to a boil
* Let simmer on medium-low heat for 90 minutes or so
* Salt and pepper to taste
